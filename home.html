"Oppenheimer," directed by the talented filmmaker Christopher Nolan, is a cinematic masterpiece that leaves a lasting impact. The film masterfully weaves together history and human emotion, drawing us into the enigmatic world of J. Robert Oppenheimer, the brilliant scientist behind the development of the atomic bomb. With stunning visuals, outstanding performances, and a thought-provoking narrative, "Oppenheimer" not only educates but also deeply engages its audience.

Some behind the scenes fun facts

  • The Trinity Test recreation was filmed without special effects
  • When in the bunker, Oppenheimer says that they'll know if the bomb is successful in 1 hour and 58 minutes. The bomb goes off in the movie at the 1 hour and 58 minute mark.
  • Actor Cillian Murphy has said that he read the Bhagavad Gita while preparing for filmmaker Christopher Nolan's 'Oppenheimer'.
